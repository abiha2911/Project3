About Me

Hi, I’m Abiha, a Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo with a passion for digital design, marketing, and fashion. Originally from Pakistan, I’ve always been drawn to the intersection of creativity, business, and technology, and I strive to bring innovative ideas to life through design and strategic thinking.

My expertise lies in graphic design, digital marketing, and branding, where I explore how visual storytelling and user experience influence consumer engagement. I’m particularly interested in how businesses can leverage digital tools to create compelling brand identities and meaningful customer interactions.

In addition to my work in design and marketing, I have a strong interest in fashion and styling. I love analyzing trends and understanding how aesthetics shape both personal expression and business strategy. Whether through branding, UX/UI design, or content strategy, I aim to create meaningful and engaging digital experiences.
